Colby Patterson

In my workshops, I always ask the students what they are having the most trouble with. Almost every single time, they answer - consistency.


Blackjack Colby Patterson played professional pool for 20 years in The United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia, becoming 9 ball champion of Mosconi Cup in 2006 and champion of champions in 2010. He has authored several books relating to the game of pocket billiards,including: "Stroke of Genius", "The Growling Point", "Building the Perfect Game", "Lessons in 9 Ball", "Chasing the Dream" etc.
